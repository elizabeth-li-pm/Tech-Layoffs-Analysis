# Tech Layoffs Analysis

Business analysis of tech industry layoffs (2020-2025): identifying patterns 
in company size, funding stage, industry, and geography to help job seekers 
assess employer risk with data instead of speculation.

**Author:** Elizabeth Li | MS Marketing Analytics, Simon Business School, University of Rochester
